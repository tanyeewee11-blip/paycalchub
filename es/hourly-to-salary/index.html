Preguntas Frecuentes

¿Cómo convierto mi tarifa por hora a salario anual? +
Multiply your hourly wage by hours a la semana, then by weeks al año. For a standard full-time job: hourly rate × 40 hours × 52 semanas = annual salary. A $25/hour rate equals $52,000 al año working full-time.
¿Cuánto equivale $15/hora al año? +
$15/hour × 40 hours × 52 semanas = $31,200 al año before taxes. After federal taxes and Social Security/Medicare, take-home pay would be approximately $25,000–$26,000 annually depending on your state.
¿Cuántas horas laborales hay en un año? +
A standard full-time schedule is 2,080 hours al año (40 hours × 52 semanas). After accounting for 10 federal holidays and 2 weeks vacation, a typical work year is closer to 1,960 horas.
