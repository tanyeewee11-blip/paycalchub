How We Calculate Home Affordability

Each estimate uses the standard 28/36 rule: housing costs should not exceed 28% of gross monthly income (conservative) or 36% (aggressive). We model three down payment scenarios (3.5%, 10%, 20%) at a 2026 mortgage rate of approximately 6.9% on a 30-year fixed loan, assuming no existing monthly debt. Use the Mortgage Calculator to model your specific situation.
