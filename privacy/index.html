1. Who We Are

PayCalcHub ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at paycalchub.com. We provide free financial calculators and educational content for US workers, job seekers, and homebuyers. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at hello@paycalchub.com or via our Contact page.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Your financial inputs are never collected. All calculations on PayCalcHub — salary, tax, mortgage, loan — run entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. The numbers you enter into our calculators are never transmitted to our servers, never stored, and never shared with any third party.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Salary, income, or wage figures you enter
  • Mortgage amounts, loan figures, or debt information
  • State or location data entered into calculators
  • Filing status, deduction amounts, or any other tax inputs

4. Google AdSense & Advertising Disclosed

PayCalcHub displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Advertising revenue allows us to keep all calculators free. Here is what this means for your privacy:

  • Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your interests and prior browsing activity.

  • We do not share any personally identifiable information with Google AdSense beyond what is collected by their standard ad scripts.
  • Your calculator inputs are never shared with Google or any advertiser.

Opt out of personalized ads: You can manage or opt out of Google's personalized advertising at google.com/settings/ads. You can also use the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool.

7. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data (e.g., contact form submissions).
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate data we hold.
  • Opt-out of advertising: Opt out of personalized ads via Google Ad Settings.
  • Data portability: Request a copy of data you've provided to us in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@paycalchub.com. We will respond within 30 days.

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at hello@paycalchub.com.

8. Children's Privacy

PayCalcHub is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected:

  • Contact form submissions: Retained for up to 12 months, then deleted.
  • Analytics data: Retained for 26 months in aggregate, anonymized form.
  • Calculator inputs: Never stored — not applicable.
