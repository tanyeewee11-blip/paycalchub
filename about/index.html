About PayCalcHub

Free, accurate financial calculators — no sign-up, no tracking, no ads before you get your answer.

PayCalcHub exists to give everyone instant, honest answers to financial questions — how much will I take home? Can I afford this house? What will my mortgage cost? We believe financial clarity shouldn't require an account, a subscription, or wading through advertising.

Every calculator is free with no hidden fees, premium tiers, or required sign-up.

🔒

100% private

All calculations happen in your browser. We never store, transmit, or sell your data.

📅

Updated for 2026

Tax brackets, contribution limits, and rates are reviewed and updated each tax year.

🌍

Multi-country

US (all 50 states), United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia — each with local tax rules.
