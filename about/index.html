About PayCalcHub

Free, accurate financial calculators — no sign-up, no tracking, no ads before you get your answer.

Our mission

PayCalcHub exists to give everyone instant, honest answers to financial questions — how much will I take home? Can I afford this house? What will my mortgage cost? We believe financial clarity shouldn't require an account, a subscription, or wading through advertising.

💰

Free forever

Every calculator on PayCalcHub is free to use, with no hidden fees, premium tiers, or required sign-up.

🔒

100% private

All calculations happen in your browser. We never store, transmit, or sell your financial data.

📅

Updated annually

Tax brackets, contribution limits, and rates are reviewed and updated at the start of each tax year.

🌍

Multi-country

We cover the US (all 50 states), United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia with country-specific tax rules.

Our calculators

PayCalcHub offers four core financial calculators, each with detailed supporting guides:

  • Salary Calculator — Take-home pay after federal, state, and FICA taxes. Covers all 50 US states, UK, Canada, and Australia. Includes 401k, health insurance, and HSA deductions.
  • Mortgage Calculator — Monthly payment, total interest, and full amortization schedule for any loan amount, rate, and term.
  • Loan Calculator — Monthly repayment and total cost for personal, auto, and student loans.
  • Hourly to Salary Calculator — Convert any hourly rate to annual, monthly, biweekly, and weekly salary equivalents.

We also publish detailed guides on home affordability by salary, monthly mortgage costs by loan amount, common hourly wage breakdowns, and annual salary to hourly conversions.
