What Does $24 an Hour Mean in Real Life?

$24 an hour is a mid-level professional wage just below the equivalent of $50,000 per year. At full-time hours, this works out to $49,920 per year gross, or about $42,149 after federal taxes — giving you roughly $3,512 per month to budget with.

$24/hour provides stable independent living in most US cities. This rate starts to enable consistent savings and emergency fund building outside of major expensive metros.
