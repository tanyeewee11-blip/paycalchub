Is $60,000 a Year a Good Salary?

$60,000 a year is above average — comfortable in most US cities, including mid-tier metros. It is above the US median individual income, placing earners in a solid middle-income bracket. $60,000 a year provides comfortable independent living in most US cities. You can typically afford a one-bedroom apartment, a car, consistent savings contributions, and occasional discretionary spending. Major coastal metros remain pricey but become more manageable.
