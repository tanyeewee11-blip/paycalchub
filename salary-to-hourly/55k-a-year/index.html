Is $55,000 a Year a Good Salary?

$55,000 a year is slightly above average — comfortable in most mid-size US cities. It is above the US median individual income, placing earners in a solid lower-middle-income bracket. After federal taxes, a single filer takes home approximately $46,231 per year — or $3,853 per month.

$55,000 a year supports comfortable independent living in most US cities outside of major coastal metros. At this income, modest savings, a car payment, and occasional travel are all achievable.
